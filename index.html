About Me

Hello, I'm Bilal Khan, a passionate Zoologist with a deep fascination for the wonders of the natural world. I hold a degree in Zoology from Abdul Wail Khan University, where I cultivated my love for understanding the intricacies of life forms around us.

With over 3 years of teaching experience, I have had the privilege of sharing my knowledge in various public and private schools up to class 10th. My commitment to education led me to work with the renowned institution TIME, where I honed my skills in imparting knowledge effectively.

I specialize in teaching a range of subjects including General Science, Biology, Chemistry, Pakistan Studies, Urdu, and Islamic Studies. I believe in making learning an interactive journey, fostering curiosity, and nurturing a love for learning in my students.

Aside from my academic pursuits, I'm fluent in English, Urdu, and Pashto, which enables me to connect with a diverse group of learners. I'm excited to embark on this online teaching journey, where I can extend my expertise and passion for the sciences to eager minds from around the world.
